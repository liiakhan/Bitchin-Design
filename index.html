Our Projects

The Book of Fashion History.

It's a redesign of the Czech book about fashion which is supposed to guide you through 200 years, specifically decades, the book itself is very comprehensive both in terms of text and images, but it was very poorly corrected in terms of copywriting. My main idea was to represent each of the selected decades in certain color tones, elements, and thus overall in the style of the specified period. Each time period begins with a title page that is created with pictorial material using a collage. In addition to changing the style of the book, the citation, overall layout and subsequent pagination was a big problem.

Jana Štefková Keramika.

Jana Štekfová is a ceramicist with a passion for experimenting with new materials inspired by nature. Jana’s work is diverse, she creates from animal urns to crockery and Venus statues, embracing czech culture. The branding aims to unify the diverse features and styles present in Jana's works. It features a coil of clay that simulates a bowl containing her name and identity as an artist. These coil elements are used constantly throughout her branding. Earthy and muted tones are featured to let the original colors of the pieces stand out.
